You travel the dungeons as the player and collect powerups. When you have killed all the enemies in the level you loop
back and kill more enemies. When you die you save one powerup. After you die you are sent to the "lobby" where you can
choose one powerup to save. Theb you run into the dungeons once more.

the gameplay loop will be: go into the dungeon -> get powerups and progress farther into the dungeon -> die -> loop

the genre of the game will be roguelike

our game will be unique because you can choose which powerup to save.

the game will be made for pc mainly but it could easily be ported to console

the game won't be story based.

the intended audience is pre-teens to teens to adults who want a fun roguelike experience

the game will be easy and light to run on most pc:s
